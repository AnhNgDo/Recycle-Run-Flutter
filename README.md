## Inspiration
This mini game is an entry for the [Global Gamers Challenge](https://flutter.dev/global-gamers) - sponsored by Flutter and Global Citizen team. 

Many of us would have heard about the importance of recycling. I wanted to reinforce this message with a game that makes recycling fun, exciting, and even heroic! I'd like players to **laugh, jump, and recycle their way to victory**, realizing that even small actions can make a big difference and learn a thing or two about the great benefits of recyclingğŸ’š
